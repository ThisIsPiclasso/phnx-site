Molly Rose Foundation

The Molly Rose Foundation was set up after the tragic loss of 14-year-old Molly Russell, who died after being bombarded with online content that normalised depression, self-harm and suicide. We’re driven by the belief that tech-facilitated self-harm and suicide can be prevented by demanding change at both a systemic, and community level.
